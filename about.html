This website teaches chinese music theory in support of
ethnomusicology. Ethnomusicology is the study of music
around the world (usually non-western).Ethnomusicologists
try to understand the process in which music in sounds are
made and how they are related to their broader social,
political and cultural context. Chinese music theory is
just a part of ethnomusicology, other systems, such as
the Japanese and Carnatic systems exist as well.
